Storage

M.2 SSD The M.2 SSD is smaller than a standard SSD and a bit faster. It's more expensive but doesn't require cables!
SSD The SSD is cheaper than the M.2 but more expensive than the HDD. It's nice to get if you want a bit more storage, but still almost as fast for cheaper.
HDD The HDD is the slowest and biggest but has the most storage out of the three. It's great for storing all of your games!
