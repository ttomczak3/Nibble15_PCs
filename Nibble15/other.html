Other

Case

The pc case is the easiest! All you have to do is make sure your motherboard will fit, looking to see if it supports Atx or Itx, for example. Other than that, make sure the airflow is good, and then you can pick how it looks.

Monitor

You should buy a monitor based on your pc build, for example, if your pc can only perform well at 1080p at 60fps get a 1080p 60hz monitor. An example is if your pc can perform well at 1440p 60 fps, I would recommend a 1080p 144hz or 1440p 60hz monitor.

Keyboard and Mouse

Gaming keyboard and mouse is a must! Because it has been proven that gaming peripherals are faster and better. Now, if you play FPS games, I would recommend putting more money towards a mouse, and if you play MOBA games, I would recommend putting it more towards the keyboard!
